Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Rezanto collects, uses, stores, and shares information when providing its package management platform.

Last updated: April 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to Rezanto's websites, software, and related services that reference this policy. It describes how we handle information for customers, property staff, residents, prospects, and website visitors.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how the Service is used, we may collect:

  • Account and contact information: names, email addresses, phone numbers, company names, property names, and job titles
  • Property and resident information: building names, unit numbers, resident names, contact details, and communication preferences
  • Package and operational data: carrier details, tracking or reference numbers, intake notes, timestamps, pickup confirmations, and related workflow records
  • Billing and transaction information: subscription details, invoices, and payment-related records
  • Technical and usage data: IP address, device/browser information, log data, and how users interact with the Service
  • Support and communications: messages, demo requests, and correspondence with Rezanto

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Enable package tracking, resident notifications, pickup workflows, and reporting
  • Manage accounts, subscriptions, invoicing, and customer support
  • Monitor performance, troubleshoot issues, and protect the security of the Service
  • Communicate about product updates, service notices, demos, renewals, and support matters
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements

4. Legal Bases and Permissions

Where required by applicable law, we process personal information based on consent, contractual necessity, legitimate interests, or legal obligation. Customers are responsible for providing any notices or obtaining any consents required for their use of resident email or SMS notifications through the Service.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information:

  • With the relevant customer or property operator to support package and resident operations
  • With service providers that help us host, secure, analyze, message, authenticate, support, or operate the Service
  • For legal or safety reasons when required by law or reasonably necessary to protect rights, property, or safety
  • As part of a business transaction such as a merger, financing, acquisition, or asset sale

6. Messaging and Notifications

Rezanto may send operational notifications by email or SMS on behalf of customers and properties using the Service. These messages may include package arrival, reminder, or pickup-related communications. Message delivery depends on third-party carriers and providers, and standard message or data rates may apply.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Service, meet contractual commitments, maintain business and security records, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and customer relationship.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Processing

Information may be processed or stored in Canada, the United States, or other jurisdictions where Rezanto or its service providers operate. When required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border data transfers.

10. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or obtain a copy of certain personal information, or to object to or restrict certain processing. You may also unsubscribe from marketing emails using the unsubscribe link in those messages. Operational service communications may still be sent when necessary.

11. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children in a manner requiring parental consent under applicable law.

12. Third-Party Sites and Services

Our website or Service may link to third-party websites, scheduling tools, login systems, or integrations. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not this one.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page and may also be communicated through the Service or by email.

15. Regional Compliance

Rezanto aims to operate in a manner consistent with applicable privacy laws, including Canadian privacy laws such as PIPEDA. If you are subject to laws that provide additional rights or impose specific requirements, please contact us and we will work with you in good faith to address applicable obligations.
