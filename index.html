Built by Industry Experts Who Understand Your Challenges

Eddie Tang

Founder & Developer

With a BSc in Computing Science from Simon Fraser University and over 15 years in enterprise B2B, Eddie has helped scale early-stage startups from first customer to successful acquisitions by industry giants including HPE (Nimble Storage), EMC (Data Domain), and Cisco (Valtix).

Throughout his career, Eddie has created applications addressing core operational challenges for enterprise customers. His deep understanding of enterprise software and ROI-driven solutions ensures Rezanto delivers real value to property managers.

The idea for Rezanto emerged during the COVID-19 pandemic when Eddie experienced firsthand the inefficiencies of manual package management in his own condo building. Watching concierge staff struggle with paper logbooks and endless phone calls, he knew there had to be a better way.

"I built Rezanto to solve a problem I lived with every day. As both the founder and lead developer, I personally ensure every customer succeeds. When you work with Rezanto, you're working directly with someone who understands both your operational challenges and the technology to solve them."
